# Chapter 10: Removing duplicates

Source: Richard Bird, *Pearls of Functional Algorithm Design*, pp. 64-72.

## Role In The Course

This pearl centers on the following problem: Remove duplicates from a list while making the performance consequences explicit.

Use it as a source for examples of calculational algorithm design in Haskell: begin with a clear executable specification, expose the cost problem, then transform the specification by laws, representation changes, or divide-and-conquer structure.
