# Chapter 17: The Knuth-Morris-Pratt algorithm

Source: Richard Bird, *Pearls of Functional Algorithm Design*, pp. 127-135.

## Role In The Course

This pearl centers on the following problem: Derive KMP string search through borders, failure functions, and automata.

Use it as a source for examples of calculational algorithm design in Haskell: begin with a clear executable specification, expose the cost problem, then transform the specification by laws, representation changes, or divide-and-conquer structure.
